What is I am a Code Ninja?

IaaCN is a silly, fun, and easy way to show to the world what you know and display your Code Ninja status!

Designed for developers of all types

Not everyone wants to design their own portfolio site. Even if you find a template you like you still have to go through the hassle of hosting it and buying a neat domain name all for what amounts to a flashy info site.

Time saver

  1. Create an account
  2. Fill out your information
  3. Choose a nice theme

That's it! We here at NinjaHQ will give you a cool domain name of {your_name}.isacode.ninja where you will show off your skills on your page!

Targeted SEO for your page

We'll take care of the basic SEO setup for your page so you can be found! Your content is key to your visibility, so really flex your brain and show off what you know!

Mobile-friendly

For easy, on-the-go consumption of your page! Be found by someone waiting for the bus!

New features being added all the time

We'll constantly release new templates and features for you to use to flex your code-fu. If you have an idea, let us know! Things can only get more awesome if you give us ideas!
